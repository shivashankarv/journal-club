## The Promise

## Why It Works

**Learning Science**:
- Multiple representations (words, equations, code, analogies)
- Interleaved difficulty (easy → hard)
- Active testing (quizzes reveal gaps)
- Hands-on building (sandbox coding)
- Spaced progression (theory → test → implement)

**Psychological**:
- Clear goals (become expert in 5-7 days)
- Visible progress (XP, levels, badges)
- Immediate feedback (tests pass/fail instantly)
- Autonomy (choose your pace)
- Mastery (build something real)

**Orchestrated**:
- No decision fatigue (system decides next steps)
- Seamless transitions (between skills)
- Personalization (adapted to your style)
- Motivation (gamification)
